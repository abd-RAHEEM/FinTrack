Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Where can I see my transaction history?

All your transactions are available in the Transactions section. You can filter them by date, category, or type.

How do I export my financial data?

Go to the Reports section and click on "Export Data". You can choose between CSV, Excel, or PDF formats.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal for premium subscriptions.

How can I delete my account?

Account deletion is available in your Profile settings under "Account Management".
